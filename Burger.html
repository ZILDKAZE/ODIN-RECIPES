Classic Burger

Description

A classic burger is a beloved dish that combines a juicy beef patty, fresh toppings, and a soft bun. It's a versatile dish that can be customized to suit different tastes and preferences.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese (optional)
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard (or your favorite condiments)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into four equal portions and shape them into patties, about 1/2 inch thick.
  3. Season both sides of the patties with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill the patties for about 4-5 minutes per side, or until they reach your desired doneness.
  5. If using cheese, place a slice on each patty during the last minute of cooking to allow it to melt.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster oven.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing the patties on the bun bottoms, followed by your desired toppings (tomato, onion, lettuce, condiments).
  8. Place the bun tops on the burgers and serve hot.
