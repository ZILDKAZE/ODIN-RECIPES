Beef and Bean Burrito

Description

A delicious and satisfying burrito filled with seasoned ground beef, refried beans, rice, and your favorite toppings. It's a hearty and flavorful meal that's perfect for any time of day.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can (15 oz) refried beans
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 8 large flour tortillas
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salsa or hot sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. In a skillet over medium-high heat, cook the ground beef until browned and crumbled. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and the recommended amount of water according to the packet instructions. Stir and simmer until thickened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Warm the refried beans in a saucepan or microwave.
  4. Warm the flour tortillas according to package instructions.
  5. To assemble the burritos, place a tortilla on a flat surface and layer with a portion of the seasoned ground beef, refried beans, cooked rice, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and shredded cheese.
  6. Fold the bottom edge of the tortilla over the filling, fold in the sides, and roll it up tightly.
  7. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and fillings.
  8. Serve the burritos warm, with salsa or hot sauce on the side if desired.
