Homemade Pizza

Description

Nothing beats a homemade pizza with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, and your favorite toppings. This recipe will guide you through making a delicious pizza from scratch.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3 cups (375g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp instant yeast or active dry yeast
  • 1 1/4 cups (295 ml) warm water (110째F/45째C)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

For the Sauce:

  • 1 (15 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Toppings:

  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced pepperoni or other desired toppings

Steps

  1. Make the dough: In a large bowl, combine the flour, salt, and yeast. Add the warm water and olive oil, and stir until a shaggy dough forms. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour.
  2. Make the sauce: In a small saucepan, combine the crushed tomatoes, basil, oregano, salt, and garlic. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Preheat your oven to 450째F (230째C) and prepare a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  4. Roll or stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle and transfer it to the prepared pan.
  5. Spread the sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border around the edges.
  6. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese and your desired toppings.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove the pizza from the oven, slice, and serve hot.
