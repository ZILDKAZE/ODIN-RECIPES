Sisig

Description

Sisig is a savory and flavorful Filipino dish made from parts of a pig's head, ears, and liver. It's a delicious and unique dish that's perfect for sharing with friends and family.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pig's ears, boiled and chopped
  • 1 lb pig's liver, boiled and chopped
  • 1 lb pig's face/cheek meat, boiled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth or water
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup calamansi or lemon juice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 Thai chili or serrano chili, sliced (adjust to desired spice level)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for frying
  • Chopped green onions for garnish

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the chopped pig's ears, liver, and face/cheek meat. Add the chicken broth or water, soy sauce, and calamansi or lemon juice. Mix well and set aside to marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a large skillet or wok, heat the butter or oil over high heat.
  3. Add the onions and garlic and saut√© until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add the marinated meat mixture and chili slices. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes, making sure to scrape the bottom of the pan to prevent sticking and burning.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Continue cooking until the meat is crispy and slightly charred, adjusting the heat as needed.
  7. Transfer the sisig to a serving plate and garnish with chopped green onions.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice and a squeeze of calamansi or lemon juice on the side.
